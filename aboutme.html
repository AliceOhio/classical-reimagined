Born in 1974 in a family with a history of musical excellence it surprised no one that I began playing piano at the age of three, continuing through to my early twenties while receiving a bachelor’s degree in music, studying under the esteemed Elizabeth Pastor.


It was there I realized the importance of not just classical music itself but in the actual performance, the musician behind the performance.


Here, in this collection, I will curate a personalized unique musical journey cultivated to your specific needs. What is stopping you from being the best you in this moment? The proper choice of music can affect your mind, your spirit, your energy and your focus.


After careful consideration and an in-depth analysis of your personal life journey and goals, I will culminate for you a collection of amazing performers, some old some new, and their recordings for your personal music experience.


Contact me for more information on how I can help you become the best you in this moment, the you that you didn’t know you could be.
